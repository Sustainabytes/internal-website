About the team!

We are a student-led community passionate about tackling environmental challenges through computational approaches. Our mission is to empower students with education, hands-on experience, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Mission Pillars

I. Upskill Through Service

Apply data science, technology, and analytics to real-world sustainability challenges

II. Build Community

Foster interdisciplinary collaborations and connect data, tech, and environmental groups through outreach, events, and creative initiatives

III. Professional Development

Engage with sustainability-focused professionals and peers through socials, networking events, speaker series, and career panels
